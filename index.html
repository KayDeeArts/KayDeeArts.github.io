About Me

Software is the new artistic medium: creatively unique, and powerfully collaborative.
After watching a TED Talk from the Pixar Director of Photography, Danielle Feinberg, I realized that the collaboration of my artistic and technological sides belonged in tech and animation.
Currently a senior at Stony Brook University, I study Computer Science with an emphasis on C, Java, Web Dev, and OpenGL, and Digital Arts with Maya, Blender, and Adobe Suites.
Outside programming but within the tech realm, I work as Curriculum Support for Girls Who Code Alumni Programs, Student Designer for the College of Engineering and Applied Sciences, and as Teaching Assistant for Introduction to Multimedia (CSE 334).
During my free time, I'm often exploring cities, taking nature walks and hanging out with family and friends.
